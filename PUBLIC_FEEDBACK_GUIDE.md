# AI Food Passport — Public Feedback Guide

> How real users, classmates, mentors, recruiters, and community members can try the public demo and provide structured feedback.

---

## 1. Purpose

This is a **public feedback collection package** for the AI Food Passport portfolio MVP. The goal is to gather honest, structured feedback on a mock-safe public demo — from real people who would actually use it.

What we want to learn:

- Does the concept make sense to first-time visitors?
- Is the UI clear and easy to navigate?
- Are the safety disclaimer and limitations honest and clear?
- Does this project demonstrate technical competence for portfolio purposes?
- What would make you more (or less) likely to trust or recommend this tool?

**This is a mock-safe MVP Alpha.** Please read these important notes before testing:

- ✅ Public web demo works — product flow is functional end-to-end
- ❌ No real OCR/AI provider is enabled — food recognition and analysis are simulated
- ❌ Not production-ready — not an App Store or TestFlight release
- ❌ No allergy safety guarantee — dietary warnings are mock data, not medical advice
- ❌ Do not use for real allergy safety decisions — this is a demonstration only
- ❌ Backend on Render free tier — may experience up to 50-second cold start on first request

## 5. Testing Scope

Try the following steps on the public web demo. No download or install needed — it runs in your browser.

### Step-by-Step Path

1. **Open the demo** — the app loads in your browser; note any loading delays (Render cold start)
2. **Set dietary preferences** — go to Settings, toggle allergens and dietary restrictions
3. **Run a mock menu analysis** — tap "Scan Menu" or equivalent; a mock dish list appears
4. **Inspect personalized allergen warnings** — check whether the badge/warning matches your saved preferences
5. **Check scan history** — verify the history screen shows your previous scans
6. **Browse privacy policy** — verify the privacy policy link is accessible and clearly worded

### What to Observe

- Did anything confuse you or take more than 5 seconds to figure out?
- Were the safety disclaimers visible and easy to understand?
- Did the allergen badge make sense for your dietary preferences?
- Did the app feel polished for a portfolio project?

## 6. Feedback Categories

We are collecting feedback in the following areas. You do not need to cover all of them — even a single sentence is valuable.

| Category | Example Questions |
|---|---|
| **First impression** | What did you think in the first 10 seconds? Did you understand what the app does? |
| **Clarity** | Was anything confusing? Were labels and instructions clear? |
| **Trust / Safety wording** | Did the safety disclaimers feel honest? Were the mock-safe limitations clear? |
| **Usefulness** | Would you use this if it had real OCR/AI? Which features felt most useful? |
| **UI / UX** | Was the interface easy to navigate? Did anything feel clunky or unfinished? |
| **Bugs or glitches** | Did anything break, freeze, or display incorrectly? |
| **Portfolio impression** | If you were a recruiter, would this project stand out? Why or why not? |

## 7. How to Report Feedback

### Option A — GitHub Issue (preferred for public visibility)

1. Go to: https://github.com/AllengWong-droid/ai-food-passport/issues
2. Click "New Issue"
3. Use label: `feedback` or `enhancement`
4. Describe what you tested and what you thought

### Option B — Direct Message

- WeChat / Line / Discord / email — whatever channel you already use to contact the builder
- No special format needed; just share your honest thoughts

### Option C — Structured Feedback Form

Use the questions in [FEEDBACK_FORM_QUESTIONS.md](FEEDBACK_FORM_QUESTIONS.md):

- **Short version** (~8 questions, 3 minutes)
- **Full version** (~20 questions, 8 minutes)

You can copy-paste your answers into a DM or GitHub issue, or send a filled form file directly.

### Option D — Anonymous Form (coming later)

A Google Form or similar anonymous collection method may be added in a future phase.
