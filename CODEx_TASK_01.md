\# AI Food Passport for Travelers



\## Project Overview



Build a production-ready Flutter iOS application.



Purpose:



Help international travelers:



\- Understand foreign menus

\- Avoid food risks

\- Discover dishes they will enjoy

\- Understand whether restaurant pricing is reasonable
